Six Gods worshipped by the Prophesized, who retained power even through the lack of faith.
- Uiy - God of Memory
- Anzer - God of War
- Esu - God of Parable
- Dicp - God of Recreation
- Nissr - God of the Dying
- Qreuit'r - God of Artistry
